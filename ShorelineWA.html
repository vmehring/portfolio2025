An existing walkway on this property provides a foundation for the planting design. Semicircular paths with a similar surface treatment provide access for maintenance and enjoying the plants. A plant palette based on ornamental grasses and evergreen shrubs, emphasizing foliage color and texture, creates a modern look. The informal arrangement and varying heights of the plants add a naturalistic touch. Plants have been selected to keep watering and pruning requirements to a minimum.
