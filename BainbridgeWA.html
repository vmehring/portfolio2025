This large, partially wooded property contains areas of full sun and deep shade. An enthusiastic property owner installed a driveway with islands for planting, drainage in the lawn, drip irrigation, and a drilled boulder fountain. The main goal for the site was to provide a dramatic visual statement when approaching the residence. An extensive plant list and a footpath through the largest planting bed echo the experience of strolling through a botanical garden. Plants are grouped according to water needs as well as exposure tolerance, with high-water-needs plants next to the residence.
